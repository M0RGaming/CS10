How to connect mics and speakers to the mixer

[This is a stand in, the actual text will be written by the head of sound for next year. However, I am somewhat knoledgeable in the arts of sound design and operation, so I will include a basic instruction of how to do stuff.] When connecting a speaker to the sound board, you will need an xlr cable. Plug the xlr cable into an input for the mixer, and voila, you have a connection. As an output, you plug one end of the xlr cable into the mixer's output. Then take the other end and plug it into the speaker. There you go, now you know how to connect the speakers to the mixer.
